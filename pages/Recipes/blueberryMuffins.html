Blueberry Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1/8 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375*F.
  2. In a mixer, cream the butter and sugar until light.
  3. Add the eggs, vanilla and milk. Mix until well-combined.
  4. Add the flour, salt and baking powder. Mix until well-combined.
  5. Fold-in the blueberries.
  6. Grease a standard muffin tin and fill with batter.
  7. Bake at 375*F for 30-35 mins
  8. Remove muffins from tin and leave them to cool for at least 30 mins. Store the muffins uncovered or else the muffins will be too moist on the second day.
