Grammy Vint's Good Bean Salad

Ingredients:

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, kidney beans, black beans, corn ,onion, and red pepper.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together vinegar, basil, olive oil, Dijon mustard, garlic, salt, hot pepper sauce and pepper.
  3. Pour contents of small bowl over contents of big bowl and toss together.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning as necessary.
  5. Garnish with chopped parsley.
  6. Serve at room temperature. You can throw this together up to one day ahead.
